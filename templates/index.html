About Us

We are a team of students who are passionate about using technology to improve people's lives. We believe that emotional well-being is just as important as physical health and that everyone deserves access to the tools and resources they need to manage their emotions effectively. That's why we created EUDA, a bot that harnesses the power of facial and vocal recognition technology to provide personalized support for emotional well-being. At EUDA, we believe that everyone has the power to take control of their emotional well-being, and we're here to help you do just that.

  • Robust Databases

    Our bot is trained with carefully curated databases like RAVDESS, TESS, SAVEE, and CREMA-D. These databases contain thousands of audio and video samples of emotional expressions from different speakers and cultures, allowing our bot to recognize and respond to a wide range of emotional states.

  • Secure Authentication

    At EUDA, we understand the importance of privacy and security. That's why we have implemented secure authentication protocols to ensure that your data is protected at all times. You can trust EUDA to provide personalized support for your emotional well-being without compromising your privacy.

  • User-friendly Interface

    Our bot is designed with an easy-to-use interface, making it simple and convenient for anyone to access the tools and resources they need to manage their emotions effectively.
